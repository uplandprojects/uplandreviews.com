I like to hunt to birds and I like to hunt often. So I need a hunting vest that can hold my gear, hold my birds, and last season after season. The Browning Bird’n Lite Vest does not disappoint. I have been using the same Bird’n Lite Vest for the past 5 seasons. It's made well and proves durable against the expected wear and tear. This vest includes enough pockets and compartments for your shells, gear, and water bottles with room to spare. It has an expandable bird bag that will easily hold your daily limit. One of the things I like the most about this vest is that it fits like a vest should. Unlike other vests that fit more like a backpack or bag. It's comfortable, easy to get on and off, and easy to clean.


Not the lightest vest available so it's less than ideal in warmer weather. But the Browning Bird’n Lite Vest is one of our favorite upland vests to be used for just about any type upland hunting in cool and cold weather conditions.

Likes

  • Well made and durable
  • Many pockets and compartments
  • Lots of space for gear
  • Easy to wash and clean

Dislikes

  • Heavy
  • Too much vest for warm weather
  • Collects twigs and brush in the open pockets

Good Buy

Great first vest that will last you years. For the price of under $200, it's a great buy.
