What we found


It's hard to imagine what scouting and hunting was like before using these hunting maps. We used to spend hours looking through state maps and marking pins on Google. With the Scout-N-Hunt you can easily identify land boundaries and habitat directly from a cell phone, tablet or computer. Spend more time chasing birds and less time scouting for spots.

Finding the right cover These maps make it extremely easy to find the cover your are looking for. From aspen cuts for ruffed grouse and woodcock, to PLOTS access and crop data for pheasant. Everything is clearly marked so you you know exactly what to expect before your hunt.

Multiple states If you like to hunt more than one state during the season, we recommend that you get the all state bundle. This gives you access to 12 states for Grouse & Woodcock and 5 state maps of Pheasant, Quail, Sharp-tails, Huns, and Chukar. With more states coming online in 2020. We went on our first ND pheasant hunt last year. Other members of our party spent hours sifting through maps online, printing them out, and marking them with up with colored pencils. Never once did we look at them. Instead we downloaded the state of ND and had all the information we needed on our phone.

Offline Chasing birds usually means limited cell service. The beauty of these maps is internet access is not needed once you download and install them to your device. The downside though is they do take up a little extra storage space. But that shouldn't be a problem for most modern phone owners.

Accuracy For the most part the cut and crop data is accurate. Although it is rare, we do find a few spots each year where the habitat does not match what is labeled on the map. It's not overly surprising since the data comes from the state. Just report the issue back to the Scout-N-Hunt team so they can go back and review the data.

One of the neat things when using the mobile hunting maps, is you find a lot of new spots you wouldn't otherwise know about. It forces you to get out and try new areas instead of hunting the same zones repeatedly throughout the season.

Scout-N-Hunt maps are a game changer

These maps contain all the essential information you need to find your next spot. Spend less time searching and more time boots on the ground hunting.
