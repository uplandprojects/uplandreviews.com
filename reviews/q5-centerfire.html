The Q5 Centerfire Upland Bird Vest is probably the most well-made vest we have seen. Constructed from extremely durable materials and yet light enough to be worn all day long. One of the more intriguing features of the product is the ability to swap out accessories and customize the vest to fit your needs. You can acquire and remove attachments such as pouches and water bottle holders in minutes. In fact, during our early season training routine we quickly realized that the external front pouches were not needed. In just a couple minutes we removed them resulting in an ultra-lite vest for early season dog training. This kind of flexibility lets you create the ideal vest for every kind of upland hunting situation.

We couldnâ€™t find much wrong with the Q5 Centerfire, except because of its durability, the vest was a little stiff with the front pouches still installed. They tended to rub on our font legs as we crouched down and moved through the grouse woods. However, with the pouches removed, the Q5 performed extremely well. In our opinion this vest is optimized for the open country. But with some adjustments it can still be used in thicker cover.

All in all this is a mighty nice vest that is made to last. With the option add and remove accessories, the Q5 Centerfire gives you the opportunity to create the perfect for every upland situation.

What we found


Quality: This vest is well made and comfortable. Comes standard with extra padding for comfort around neck and upper back. Back is solid to avoid snagging while the inner portion is mesh for maximumn ventilation. It has a padded hip-belt system with lumbar support for effective weight distribution and puts load on the hips, freeing your upper body for unrestricted movement.

Adjustable: One thing you will notice with the Q5 product is everything is adjustable and customizable. You can add and remove pouches and compartments at will. This is allows you to adjust to the type of hunting and terrain. The downside with this setup is there is little room for accessory compartments that come standard. We would have loved to see some more D-rings and pockets without having to purchase additional accessories.

Made for open country: If you are hunting the open country of the western plains, the Q5 is the perfect vest for you. Plenty of storage and lightweight to go all day long. If you hunt the grouse woods of the midwest, we recommend you keep the customizations and compartments to a minumum. We found the more accessories you have the more constricting and bulky the Q5 becomes. Making it hard to navigate thick cover. Once we removed those extra pouches, we had no problem getting around.

Pro Tips

To make the Q5 Centerfire better to maneuver in the woods, try removing one or both pouches. It makes it easier to bend down or crawling over brush.



The inside of the bird pouch contains a pocket on each side. Making it ideal for smaller water bottles for both you or your dogs.
