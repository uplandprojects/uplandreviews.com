D.T. Systems Remote Bird Launcher

Summer 2020
Previous Next

The BL Series includes an "Easy-Load" trap door making safe and easy to load birds. The quite release system provides a more natural flush that prevents spooking your dog. This remote bird launcher includes large scent holes, remote range up to 700 yards, and a beeper locator to make the receiver easy to find. You can scale up by adding up to 16 receivers to single remote.

The BL 505 series is made for quail/pigeon size birds while the BL 705 is better for larger birds such as pheasant or ducks.

What we found


Control: The BL series is a great tool for getting young dogs bird exposer while providing a controlled environment. Make sure you invest in the remote launcher instead of the manual. It provides full control from afar allowing you to setup your dog for success.

Sizing: We do suggest that, unless you are going to specifically launch pheasants, you should go with the BL 509 series. The BL 709 is too much launcher for anything smaller. It becomes heavy to hall around the in the field. It's larger size also makes it difficult to hide in thin to moderate cover. The BL 509 is the right choice for most training situations.

Easy-Load: Our favorite feature with these launchers is how easy they are to load. The "easy-load" system provides an easy access door that is used to slip the bird into the launcher after it has been set. With the safety pin in place, you never have to worry about an accidental release.

That said the safety pin is secured by a small lightweight chain that tends to break easily. Sadly ours disappeared after only a couple weeks of use. We recommend securing it with something stronger than what is provided.

Must have for anyone training young dogs

The BL 505 and the BL 705 are great bird launchers for both pro and amateur dog trainers. They are easy to use, safe, quiet, and dependable.
